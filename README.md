## Getting Started

### Step 1: Access the Website

Open your browser and go to:
```
http://localhost/digital_music_store/digital_music_store/index.php
```

### Step 2: Create an Account

1. Click **"Register"** in the top menu
2. Fill in your details:
   - **Full Name**
   - **Email Address**
   - **Password**
   - **Choose Role:**
     - **Listener** - Browse and enjoy music
     - **Artist** - Upload and manage your music
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. You'll be redirected to login

### Step 3: Login

1. Click **"Login"** in the menu
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password**
3. Click **"Login"**

## For Listeners

### Browse Music
- The **homepage** displays all available music in a grid
- Each music card shows:
  - Cover image
  - Title and Artist
  - Genre and Price
  - Audio player (if available)
- Click the **play button** ▶️ on any song to listen

### Search for Music
- Use the **search bar** at the top of the page
- Type any keyword (title, artist, genre)
- Results update instantly as you type

### Filter by Genre
- Scroll down to the **"Browse by Genre"** section
- Click any genre tag (Afrobeat, Gospel, Hip-Hop, etc.)
- (This will filter the displayed music)

### Listen to Songs
- Each song has an **audio player** at the bottom of the card
- Click the **play button** ▶️ to start listening
- Use the player controls to pause, adjust volume, or skip

### View Music Details
- Each card shows:
  - Title
  - Artist name
  - Genre badge
  - Price
  - Audio player

## For Artists

### Upload New Music

1. Login as an **Artist**
2. Click **"Add Music"** in the menu (or sidebar)
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Title** - Song name
   - **Artist** - Your name or band
   - **Genre** - Select from dropdown
   - **Price** - Set your price (in USD)
   - **Description** - Brief description
   - **Cover Image** - Upload album art
   - **Audio File** - Upload MP3/WAV/OGG
   - **Release Date** - When it was released
4. Click **"Add Music"**
5. Your music will appear on the homepage!

### Manage Your Music

#### Edit Music
1. Find your music on the homepage
2. Click the **"Edit"** button on your music card
3. Update any details
4. Click **"Update Music"**

#### Delete Music
1. Find your music on the homepage
2. Click the **"Delete"** button
3. Confirm deletion

### **Important Artist Notes**
- You can only **edit/delete your own** music
- Other artists' music shows as **"Owned by [Artist]"**
- Admin can edit/delete any music

## 👑 **For Admins**

### **Access Admin Panel**

After logging in as Admin, you'll see additional menu items:
- 👥 **Users** - Manage all users
- 🎵 **Music** - Manage all music
- 📩 **Messages** - View contact messages

---

### **User Management (👥 Users)**

#### **View All Users**
- Click **"Users"** in the menu
- See all registered users in a table

#### **Search Users**
- Use the **search bar** at the top
- Search by **email** or **name**
- Click **"Search"**

#### **Add a New User**
- Click **"Add User"**
- Fill in:
  - Full Name
  - Email
  - Password
  - Role (Listener, Artist, Admin)
- Click **"Create User"**

#### **Edit a User**
- Click the **Edit** button on any user
- Update:
  - Name
  - Email
  - Role
  - Password (optional)
- Click **"Update User"**

#### **Block a User**
- Click the **Lock** button on a user
- The user will be blocked from logging in
- They'll see "Your account has been blocked"

#### **Unblock a User**
- Click the **Unlock** button on a blocked user
- The user can now log in again

#### **Delete a User**
- Click the **Delete** button on a user
- Confirm deletion
- ⚠️ This permanently removes the user

---

### **Music Management (🎵 Music)**

#### **View All Music**
- Click **"Music"** in the menu
- See all music (including blocked)

#### **Search Music**
- Use the **search bar** at the top
- Search by:
  - Title
  - Artist
  - Creator name

#### **Block a Song**
- Click the **Block** button on any song
- The song will show as **"🚫 Blocked by Admin"**
- Listeners and Artists can't see it

#### **Unblock a Song**
- Click the **Unblock** button on a blocked song
- The song becomes visible again

#### **Delete a Song**
- Click the **Delete** button on any song
- Confirm deletion

---

### **Message Management (📩 Messages)**

#### **View Messages**
- Click **"Messages"** in the menu
- See all messages from the contact form

#### **Message Status**
- 🔴 **Unread** - New messages
- 📖 **Read** - You've read them
- ✅ **Replied** - You've replied

#### **Mark as Read**
- Click **"Mark Read"** on an unread message
- Status changes to "Read"

## ❓ **Need Help?**

### **Common Issues**

#### **"I forgot my password"**
- Contact the Admin to reset your password
- Admin can edit your password in user management

#### **"I can't upload music"**
- Check file size (max 10MB)
- Check file format (MP3, WAV, OGG)
- Make sure you're logged in as an Artist

#### **"My music is blocked"**
- Contact the Admin
- Only Admin can block/unblock music

#### **"I can't log in"**
- Check your email and password
- Contact Admin if your account is blocked
